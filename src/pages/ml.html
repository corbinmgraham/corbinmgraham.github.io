Checkers AI, 2022

Play this AI in checkers and see if you can beat it.

Description

This Checkers AI is using decision trees and Alpha-Beta Pruning to adjust its game and counter your moves. The assessment algorithm is using the measurement of "greatest jump count", or the total number of possible jumps at any time. Other measurements are possible but often, the greatest number of jumps will result in the advantage. All other moves are pruned. If there are multiple options with the same jump count, Alpha-Beta pruning is used to assess the best outcome of each option.
This AI has an excellent runtime due to its Alpha-Beta pruning and measurement algorithm.

Auto-Grader NLP, Ongoing

Why grade homework yourself?

Description

Have you ever noticed how outdated Scantron scanners and forms are? Or how they are only formatted for one type of assignment? This ease of grading has led the entire education system to ditch the ideas of creativity and allowing students to construct new and creative ideas for an easier way to grade assignments.
The Auto-Grader NLP is the solution to this issue. Using modern NLP, and statisitics, it's possible to predict the accuracy of a student's answer in many forms of assignments. This will remove the possibility of bias from the professor or grader of the assignment while also reducing the time it takes to grade assignments.
